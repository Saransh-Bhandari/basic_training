How to reply to email and chat ?
To reply to an email click to reply button on the bottom of the screen. You can send the chats to any number of emails and they can reply on same or different email.

What skills should you have to write a perfect email ?
The email should be clear and concise with proper greeting and sign-off, and well formatted with no grammatical errors.

What is Art of Proofreading ?
Proofreading means to carefully review the material for spelling, punctuation error to ensure the content is professional.

What do you mean by being responsive?
Being responsive means to reply with proper information without wasting senders time.

Is formatting your email necessary ?
Yes, formatting is important to increase readability of the email to convey the message clearly and convey professionalism.
