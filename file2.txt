What is an experiment? 
It is a test that is done under controlled conditions to test or prove something or discover new knowledge.

What are POCs? 
POCs are small projects that are created to test, evaluate and demonstrate an idea in real world scenario.

How to report your findings? 
Report of findings should involve several steps such as organizing the data (steps, other documents of reference) and their results in a well formatted and logical manner to communicate everything effectively.
